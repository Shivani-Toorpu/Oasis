About Us

Oasis School of Excellence is a co-educational, English medium secondary school affiliated with CBSE, located at Nagarguda - Shamshabad Rd, Rangareddy, Telangana. As a leading day boarding school in the heart of Telangana, Oasis School of Excellence offers a unique educational experience that combines academic excellence, personal growth, and social development. Conveniently situated, the school is easily accessible to families in the surrounding areas, making it a preferred choice for quality education. Our day boarding program provides students with the flexibility to excel academically while supporting families in maintaining a balanced work-life schedule. Students benefit from extended hours for academic studies, extracurricular activities, and social interaction with peers. Our experienced and dedicated faculty create a nurturing learning environment that encourages critical thinking, creativity, and collaboration. With a range of academic programs, including honors and Advanced Placement courses, students are equipped to challenge themselves and prepare for college and future success. At Oasis School of Excellence, we are committed to fostering well-rounded individuals, ensuring every student achieves their full potential in a supportive and engaging environment.
